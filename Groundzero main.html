Groundzero Engineering Ltd.

Construction, Design And Procurement Services

Who we are.

Ground Zero Engineering Ltd is an Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) company that specializes in integrated environmental projects, particularly in mechanical, civil (foundation/structural), and electrical engineering. With expertise in metal and structural fabrications, the company provides comprehensive design and construction services for oil and gas storage facilities, including both surface and underground tanks, as well as associated pipe works and dispensary systems. Committed to delivering top-quality, tailored solutions for clients, Ground Zero Engineering emphasizes operational excellence and continuous improvement to ensure flawless project outcomes.
